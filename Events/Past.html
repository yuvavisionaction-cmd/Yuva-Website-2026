Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about our past events and how to access resources

How can I view photos from past events?

Visit our Photo Gallery to browse through albums from all our past events. You can also find event-specific photo links in the event details modal.

Are event recordings available?

Yes! Many of our events are recorded and available on our Events Recordings page. You can watch highlights, full sessions, and keynote speeches from past events.

Can I get a certificate for events I attended?

Certificates are typically sent to registered participants via email within 7-10 days after the event. If you haven't received yours, please contact us at events@yuva.org with your registration details.

How can I stay updated on future events?

Subscribe to our newsletter below, follow us on social media, or visit our Upcoming Events page regularly to stay informed about new events, workshops, and programs.

Can I share past event details with others?

Absolutely! Click the "Share" button on any event card to share event details on WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Telegram, or copy the direct link to share anywhere.
